SMART COVID MONITORING SYSTEM

SMARTCARE brings an integration of style, technology,portability and safety, all in one design

DESCRIPTION

The product is an innovative technology incorporation designed to curb the spread of Corona virus in both the covid and post covid era. It is designed to be worn at a strategic position in the body which is the wrist, a positioning which enables the device to function at 100% efficiency. The product supervises the actions of the hands (the main spreader of the virus) and keeps a log of it, sensing hand movement and body temperature, indications given whenever the hands come close to the face or whenever the body temperature is above or below normal. These features possessed by the product enable both disease control units and personnel wearers, keep tab.

HOW IT WORKS

SmartCare through Bluetooth wireless communication technology and array of collection of well engineered network of sensors is able to monitor the user's arms movement, monitor user's body temperature and also communicate with nearby devices of its kind, so as to ensure proper social distancing. Through the bluetooth technology we are able to as well connect SmartCare to our smart phones for a better usage experience.

THE DEVICE

Taking portability as a major factor, we came up with a pair of device worn on the wrist, SMARTCARE. SmartCare has:
  • The main control wrist watch
  • An auxiliary wrist band

Features and functions

These features were put in place to reduce the spread of the virus. These features incorporated in the device are

  • The ability to sense once one is about touching his or her face by taking note of the degree at which the arm has bent
  • The ability to constantly monitor the temperature of the user. This is of importance in this covid era so one can know when to self isolate and seek testing when the body temperature is abnormal
  • The ability to help maintain the social distancing proposed guide. Using capacitive proximity sensor, the device can sense how close the wearer is to people around so he or she can maintain social distancing
  • Working with the national disease control sector in the country the user is, the device will be able to alert the authority when one show persistent abnormality in temperature
